About Us

We are a company with decades of experience in the panel industry, specializing in cool room construction and fabricating portable buildings. Our journey has been marked by significant projects, including contributions to some of the largest construction jobs in the world. We are proud of our legacy and our continuous growth in the construction industry.

1987 Aug. 20

Began learning in the panel industry by working with some of Australia's biggest companies including Pacesetter, Portacom, Ausco, and James Hardies.

1998 Sept. 5

Started installing various cool rooms and freezers around the country.

1995 Mar. 5

Contracted for all major sandwich panel building companies, we took a keen interest in cool room construction.

2000 July 9

Partnered with one of Australia's largest manufacturers of switch rooms, temperature-controlled shelters, and transportable rooms. Close to 9000 sandwich panel buildings have been manufactured by CMT Panels with our partner.

2003 Nov. 12

We became what we are now known as: CMT Panels.

2009 Aug. 25

Began our involvement in the Gorgon project, Barrow Island WA, one of the largest construction jobs in the world at that time.

2011 Aug. 03

Moved to Thomaston in Victoria to accommodate larger projects and expand.

2012 Jan. 24

Began our involvement in the Wheatstone project, Onslow WA, one of the largest construction jobs in the world.

2014 Apr. 28

Helped with the relocation of the South Melbourne Market.

2017 +

Our company continues to grow and make its mark in the construction industry.
